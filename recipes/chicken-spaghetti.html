Chicken Spaghetti

Description

A rich hearty meal. Easy to make and great for leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 8oz angel hair pasta
  • 2 cups chopped cooked chicken
  • 2 (10oz) cans cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning

Directions

  1. preheat oven to 350F degrees
  2. cook pasta per box directions then drain well
  3. add cooked pasta, chopped chicken, cream of chicken soup, salsa, sour cream, 1 cup Mexican cheese, and taco seasoning to pot
  4. stir for a few minutes until combined
  5. pour mixture to 9x13 pan
  6. top mixture with remaining Mexican cheese
  7. cover mixture with foil
  8. cook in preheated oven for about 25 minutes
