Butterscotch Cheesecake

Descritpion

A rich dessert. My dad's favorite recipe for special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups finely crushed graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 2/3 cups butterscotch chips
  • 4 packages room temperature cream cheese
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4 large egs slightly beaten
  • 1/4 cup caramel ice cream topping
  • whipped topping

Directions

  1. preheat oven to 325F degrees
  2. wrap outside bottom and side of 9 inch springform pan in heavy duty foil
  3. combine graham cracker crumbs, butter and 1/4 cup sugar in medium bowl and press into bottom and side of springform pan
  4. set aside 2 tablespoons of butterscotch chips
  5. heat milk and remaining butterscotch chips in saucepan over low heat until chips are melted then remove from heat and cool to room temperature
  6. beat cream cheese, remaining sugar, flour and vanilla extract until combined
  7. add butterscotch mixture and beat until smooth
  8. add eggs and beat until just combined
  9. pour mixture into prepared pan
  10. add pan of water to oven or cups of water on each side of springform pan while in oven
  11. bake for 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 hours or until 2 inch area around outside edge appears set when gently shaken
  12. cool slowly over several hours
  13. cover and refrigerate overnight
  14. spread caramel topping over top of cheesecake and decorate with whipped topping and remaining butterscotch chips
